About Tamilnadu Tourism

About us

Tamilnadu Tourism Development Corporation (TTDC) was incorporated in 1971 with the main object of promoting tourism in Tamilnadu by building tourism related infrastructure on commercial basis. TTDC made a modest begining by taking over 5 Govt. bungalows and operated 2 coaches. It has made rapid strides since then and today it has earned the proud distinction of owning the longest chain of hotels in South India numbering 53. The fleet of coaches has increased to 12. TTDC is providing the following facilities covering all the major tourists destinations in the state for the benifit of tourists.
